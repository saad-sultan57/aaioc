What sets us apart from all other
current competitions?

Every year, you will be able to build new bridges of communication and develop a network of relationships between participants and actors in the olive oil sector (importers, hotels and restaurants chains, chefs…) through your presence or the presence of your representatives in the host country.

You can market your products to clients and businesses of all participating countries.

We will announce the results by inviting many businesses and individuals who work in the sector or are generally interested in olive oil from a business perspective,especially: suppliers, famous chefs and representatives of chains of hotels and restaurants.

Our experts will be completely ready to inform you as a participant about the properties of the oils preferrable to the host country’s people and market, which will help you better target the segment you want to market your product to..

The market you can target through your participation with us is the largest ever, the one growing the most demographically and economically.

You will be able to provide people with your brand information – by promoting a brand influencer story for people to remember and share, for example. Announcing the brand name of the company and its products.
