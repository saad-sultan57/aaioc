About AAIOOC

Afro-Asian International Olive Oil Competition

AAIOOC Afro-Asian International Olive Oil Competition is a new innovative idea by Biotech. & Process Engineer R. Chouket that aims to reach distant points and countries of the globe, with the results being announced in one of the capitals of those countries – much like a World Cup of olive oil in its formation and preparation. We like to keep things fresh and so we will bring the contest worldwide.AAIOOC Afro-Asian International Olive Oil Competition is the first international competition for extra virgin olive oil, that several countries in both Africa and Asia participate in its organization, and the results are announced every year in one of the capitals of those countries, which makes us one of the most far-reaching and international competitions of this kind.
